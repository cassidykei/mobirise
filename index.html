ABOUT ME

"Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts." - Unknown

I have always loved telling stories. Ever since I was young I liked to hear fantastic tales of fantasy and fiction. But as I grew up I learned that the best stories are written with a truth behind them.


So I pursued journalism, the art of true storytelling. From there, my writing interests have grown into business reporting, local news coverage, national event coverage, and video game journalism.


I truly believe in the power of verbal, written and visual storytelling.

I was born in China, adopted with my twin sister, by my American parents at three months old. Raised in Arizona, I have come to appreciate the heat and all the beauty of the desert.


When I'm not binge-watching an entire Netflix Original, you can probably find me writing, coding or photographing something.


Issues I am most passionate about are women's health, adoption and education.
